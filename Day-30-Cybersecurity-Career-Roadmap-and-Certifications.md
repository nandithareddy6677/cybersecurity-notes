# 📖 Introduction

Cybersecurity is one of the fastest-growing technology fields.

Professionals protect organizations from cyber threats by securing networks, applications, cloud environments, and sensitive data.

A successful cybersecurity career requires continuous learning, hands-on practice, certifications, and real-world experience.

# ❓ Interview Questions

### 1. Name three cybersecurity career paths.

- SOC Analyst
- Cloud Security Engineer
- Penetration Tester

---

### 2. What certification is recommended for beginners?

ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) or CompTIA Security+.

---

### 3. Why is GitHub important?

It demonstrates practical skills and showcases projects to recruiters.

---

### 4. Why should cybersecurity professionals continue learning?

Because cyber threats and technologies evolve constantly.

---

### 5. Which programming language is most commonly used in cybersecurity?

Python.
